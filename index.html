Our Story

We are a group of passionate miners who do not just want to enjoy rewards but have a great time and experience. Ever since the beginning we have been operating mining on some Proof-of-Work chains.

For a while, we've seen miners frustrated with the lack of funds, tools and data in the mining ecosystem. Existing tools are hard to find and even harder to own one. The lack of established information in the industry has led miners and investors to invest in hardware with inaccurate revenue and mis-utilization of hardware, which has certainly led to loss of hard-earned money.

This is where MrGreenMiners came into existence breaking the barrier and providing the opportunities for newcomers, interested home miners, as well as investors. Simplifying the mining ecosystem, making it accessible and fun for everyone, with minimum investment. We will continue to work with the same passion, enthusiasm and expertise.
